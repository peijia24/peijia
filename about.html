Hi there, I'm Peijia!

I'm an UX/UI designer who is currently taking a gap year from UBC Cognitive Systems to take up more technical skills at BCIT!

Studying the relationships between intelligent systems and having intellectual discourse at university was fun, but it was also slow-paced (and dare I say even boring at times?), but UX/UI design offered me a real-life application in what I wanted to learn more about - translating a human experience to a digital world. It also gave me a re-introduction to art and design, something that I ignored for years (because you don't want to end up as a starving artist as my parents would tell me...Anyways, I hope I don't...)

I also moonlight as a florist in my free time ☞ and I avidly enjoy lattes, content creation and travelling along rural villages in Asia.
