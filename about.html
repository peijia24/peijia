So here's the deal.

I'm Peijia, and I've just finished a gap year to figure out my life. Actually it was a really great break from school, and I learned a whole lot about myself, including my passion for UX design! 🍊

I see design as a way of solving problems holistically, by both making something enjoyable yet intuitive and functional to what the solution needs to be.

I entered university with a fixed mindset of doing something traditional like law or teaching, so I never gave much thought about the digital realm. However, I found myself leaning towards psychology and geography, because both involved understanding more about how we thought and how we interact with the environment + society.

I then ended up in Cognitive Systems because it was so cool to see how we can apply knowledge from living systems, like how humans process information, to artificial systems - which was how I found UX design. Currently, I'm exploring graphic design, film photography and living with intentionality.

Anyways, thanks for reading my story ^ and say hi! I’d love to grab a coffee chat with you! ☺
