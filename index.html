UBC Launchpad: Room8

Sept 2018 - present; launching summer 2019

Room8 is an in progress android app aimed at creating a platform between roommates and/or homeowners who are renting out their property. By creating a niche space where rules, contracts, and tasks could be negotiated and assigned between strangers, we hope to make the adjustment to adult life a little bit easier.

Designed and developed by a group of students in UBC Launchpad.
